Click Me! (User Test)

· Thank you ·

Welcome tester! First, thank you for taking the time to test out my final project. This website is a "thank you" to my family and friends who have supported me throughout my univeristy journey. Every person who is highlighed here has impacted my life. I can't say thank you enough because without them, I truly would not be where I am today. This project is dedicated to them. ❤

I would greatly appreciate if you would perform the following tasks and tell me your opinion:
1. Scroll through each section and ensure that the background color changes.
2. Hover over the "thank you(s)" and click on them. When you click on a thank you, it should automatically scroll to that section (person).
3. Click on the image of each section and a personalized "thank you" card to that person should appear. I have not yet written the messages, thus you will see a random message as a placeholder.
