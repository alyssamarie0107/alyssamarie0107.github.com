About Website

· Thank you ·

This website is a "thank you" to my family and friends who have supported me throughout my univeristy journey. Many people have helped me get to this point in my life and I wish I could show every single person. However, for the sake of space, I chose a handful of people who have impacted my life and can't imagine life without them. I can't thank them enough because I truly would not be where I am today without them. This project is dedicated to them. ❤

My Family

· Thank you ·

To my family, I truly can't thank you guys enough. You all witnessed my late nights studying or doing homework, you all witnessed my tears when I was stressed or overwhelmed. You all witnessed my relief and smile when I passed a test or class. You guys witnessed it all. What all those moments have in common is that you were all there to support, celebrate, and lift me up in every way possible. Without you all, this accomplishment of graduating would not have been possible. This is not just for me, but I did it all for you guys as well. Thank you for the constant support and love.

Love always, Alyssa

Catalina Navarro

· Thank you ·

To my best friend, "partna", and sister, Cat, there are no words that do justice that express my gratitude for you and our friendship. You have kept me afloat through it all. It's honestly not an overstatement to say that you've saved me. I would not have been able to do this without you, so from the bottom of my heart, thank you. Living with you at 339 has been one of the best things in my life. You are one of the main reasons why my time at Davis is so memorable.

As this chapter in our lives comes to a close, I am excited and thankful to continue to live with you in our dream city, San Francisco. I couldn't ask for a better person to be "adulting" with. I love you bro. Thank you again for everything. P.S "you stink".

Love always, Alyssa (aka Leemie)
